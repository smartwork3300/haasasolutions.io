About Shree Haasa

Shree Haasa is a staffing agency that represents a wide array of creative and allied talent which includes: Graphic Designers (web and print), copywriters, art directors, creative directors, producers, project managers, traffic and studio managers, media buyers, video editors, web programmers etc., It can be Full-time, Temporary staffing and contract based. Shree Haasa attempts to meet all the requirements and conduct in-depth portfolio & employment history reviews to understand talent strengths and weaknesses
